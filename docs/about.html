Who We Are

ChessTest is an independent and thriving free online chess server, dedicated to providing an enjoyable and accessible platform for chess enthusiasts around the world. Founded in 2015 by Bob Chess, an avid chess player and visionary, ChessTest has grown to become a vibrant community of players who share a passion for the game. Our commitment to fair play, inclusivity, and continuous improvement sets us apart in the online chess world.

Our Mission

At ChessTest, our mission is simple yet profound: to make chess accessible to all. We believe in the power of chess to bring people together, fostering strategic thinking, intellectual growth, and a sense of community. Whether you're a seasoned grandmaster or just starting to learn the game, ChessTest provides a welcoming environment where everyone can enjoy the beauty and challenge of chess.
